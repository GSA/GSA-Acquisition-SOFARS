5606.302-1 Only One Responsible Source

(Added August 2015)

The waiver authority identified in DFARS PGI 206.302-1(d) is the Director of Procurement. The request for information (RFI) or source sought notice should be accomplished during market research and documented in the J&A. The RFI or sources sought is separate from the presolicitation notice required by FAR 5.2.

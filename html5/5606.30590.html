5606.305-90 Public Disclosure of J&A Documents for Noncompetitive Contracts.

(Added June 2010)

In accordance with FAR 6.305 , contracting officers are required to post J&As for noncompetitive contracts to theContract Opportunities in beta.SAM .

(c)Contracting officers must ensure that an adequate Operational Security (OPSEC) review was conducted prior to posting.

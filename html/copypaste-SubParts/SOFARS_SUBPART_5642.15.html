Subpart 5642.15 - CONTRACTOR PERFORMANCE INFORMATION

Parent topic: Part 5642 - CONTRACT ADMINISTRATION AND AUDIT SERVICES

5642.1503 Procedures.

(Revised June 2021)

(d)

(1) USSOCOM HCDs and FCOs shall use the Contractor Performance Assessment Reporting System ( CPARS ) as the method to record evaluations of contractor performance.

(2) Those contractor performance evaluations involving national security/classified contract actions shall not be entered into CPARS; however, they must follow CPARS reporting requirements in conjunction with the individual program security requirements.

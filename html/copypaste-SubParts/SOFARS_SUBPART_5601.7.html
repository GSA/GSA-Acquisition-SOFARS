Subpart 5601.7 - DETERMINATIONS AND FINDINGS

(Added June 2018)

Parent topic: Part 5601 - THE FEDERAL ACQUISITION REGULATION SYSTEM

5601.704 Content.

(Revised November 2018Unless otherwise noted in the SOFARS or DCG, there is no prescribed format or form for determinations and findings (D&Fs); however, D&Fs are required to contain the information specified in FAR 1.704 and any information that is required by the specific FAR or DFARS section under which the D&F is issued.

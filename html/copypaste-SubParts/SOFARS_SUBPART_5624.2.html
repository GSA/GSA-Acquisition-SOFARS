Subpart 5624.2 - FREEDOM OF INFORMATION ACT

Parent topic: Part 5624 - PROTECTION OF PRIVACY AND FREEDOM OF INFORMATION

5624.203 Policy.

(Revised June 2021)

Each contracting office shall utilize their component's Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) office process. Contracting offices may contact USSOCOM Command Information Services Division FOIA Manager (SOCS-SJS-VI) for additional assistance.

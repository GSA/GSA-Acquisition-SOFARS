5616.307 Contract Clauses.

(Revised November 2007)

(j) Contracting officers shall insert a clause substantially the same as 5652.216-9000, Fixed Fee Information, in all cost plus fixed-fee type solicitations and contracts.

(k) Contracting officers may insert 5652.216-9002, Withholding of Fixed Fee or Incentive Fee, in cost-plus fixed fee or cost-plus incentive fee contracts where a reduced withholding percentage is negotiated in accordance with FAR 52.216-8, "Fixed Fee," or FAR 52.216-10, "Incentive Fee."

(l) Contracting officers shall insert a clause substantially the same as 5652.216-9005, Government Down Time for Various Reasons, including the President and the Secretary of Defense, in solicitations and contracts when award of a cost type contract is anticipated.
